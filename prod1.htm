DRAGON FRUIT

DESCRIPTION:

 The dragon fruit, also known as pitaya, is a vibrant and exotic fruit with a captivating appearance and a sweet, tangy flavor. Its bright red, yellow, or even purple skin, covered in scales, is a striking sight, while the flesh inside is a creamy white speckled with tiny black seeds. This unique combination of colors and textures makes it a popular choice for both culinary and decorative purposes. The taste of dragon fruit is often described as a blend of kiwi and pear, with a subtle sweetness and a slightly tangy aftertaste. It's a refreshing and healthy fruit, packed with vitamins C, B1, and B2, as well as minerals like iron and phosphorus. It's also low in calories and high in fiber, making it a nutritious and delicious snack or ingredient for various dishes.

WHERE:

 Davao Region at SOCCSKSARGEN (South Cotabato, Cotabato, Sultan Kudarat, Sarangani, at General Santos City).
